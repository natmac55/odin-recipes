Chicken and Rice

My famous healthy chicken and rice!

This is my nice healthy food for when im trying to stick to a diet

Ingredients

  • 250g Chicken Breast, sliced into small chunks.
  • 100g Basmatie Rice, unsliced.

Steps

  • Start by boiling a pan of water, slightly salted.
  • Add 100g of Basmati rice.
  • This needs to boil for around 10-15 minutes.
  • Fry 250g of chicken breast in a small amount of oil.
  • Turn the chicken until golden brown.
  • Drain the rice, serve with the chicken on top.
  • Garnish with plenty of salt and pepper, maybe even MSG!
